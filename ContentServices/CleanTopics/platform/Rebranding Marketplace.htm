Administrators on the master tenant can rebrand the Marketplace by adding a custom logo to replace the Infinite Blue Platform logo. From the application switcher, navigate to the Marketplace application's setup page. The setup page for the Marketplace application opens. Click Edit. In the Custom Logo field, click Choose File. Browse to the file and click Open. Note: The custom logo file must be a .gif, .jpg, or .png file. Click Save. Refresh the Marketplace page. Marketplace is rebranded with your custom logo:
