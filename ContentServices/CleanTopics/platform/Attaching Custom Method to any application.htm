To attach custom method to any application: From the application switcher drop-down available next to the application name : To navigate to settings for the current application, click App Settings. To navigate to settings for a different application, hover your mouse pointer over the application and click its associated Application Settings icon. Scroll down to the Custom Methods section. Click Attach Custom Methods. From the available list of Custom Methods, select the methods you want to attach. When you finish adding methods, click Save. When exporting an application, the custom methods attached to the application is included with application.xml. When importing an application, the attached custom methods are displayed in the tenant's custom method section. Note: Deselecting a custom method is not allowed while importing an application xml.
