Administrative users can configure the calendar to specify the default view and the range of hours to display. To configure the calendar: In the calendar toolbar, click configure: The Configure Calendar Component window opens. Configure the desired properties: Default View: Day, Work Week, Week, Month, or Agenda) From Hour: Starting hour (0 to 10) to display on Day, Work Week, and Week views To Hour: Starting hour (17 to 24) to display on Day, Work Week, and Week views Click Save.
